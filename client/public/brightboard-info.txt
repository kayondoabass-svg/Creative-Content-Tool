## About BrightBoard
Website: https://www.brightboardapp.com
Title: BrightBoard - AI Content for Teachers
Company: Keyo Technologies
Support Email: support@brightboardapp.com
Description: BrightBoard is an AI-powered educational content creation platform built for teachers. It helps educators save time by instantly generating high-quality classroom materials using artificial intelligence.

## What BrightBoard Does
BrightBoard allows teachers to create:
- AI-generated educational images and illustrations
- Complete slide presentations with speaker notes
- Printable worksheets with questions and fill-in-the-blanks
- Animated video storyboards frame-by-frame
- Stories, explanations and learning materials
- Mind maps for brainstorming and concept exploration
- Activity plans for any grade level and subject

## Who It's For
BrightBoard is designed for teachers, educators, tutors, homeschoolers, and school administrators who want to save time creating classroom content. It works for all grade levels and subjects.

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I get started?
A: Sign up for free at https://www.brightboardapp.com. You get free generations to try all tools before subscribing.

Q: What AI model does BrightBoard use?
A: BrightBoard uses OpenAI GPT-4o for text and content generation, and gpt-image-1 for image generation.

Q: Can I cancel my subscription?
A: Yes, you can cancel anytime from your account settings. No long-term commitment required.

Q: Is BrightBoard suitable for all subjects?
A: Yes. BrightBoard works for all subjects including Math, Science, English, History, Art, Languages, and more.

Q: How long does it take to generate content?
A: Images and text generate in seconds. Presentations may take up to 2 minutes depending on complexity.

Q: Can schools get a group plan?
A: Yes. The Institution Plan at $100/month is designed for schools and businesses with multiple teachers. Contact support@brightboardapp.com for onboarding.

Q: What languages are supported?
A: BrightBoard can generate content in multiple languages depending on the prompt you provide.

Q: How do I become an affiliate?
A: Visit https://www.brightboardapp.com/affiliate to join the affiliate program and get your referral link.
