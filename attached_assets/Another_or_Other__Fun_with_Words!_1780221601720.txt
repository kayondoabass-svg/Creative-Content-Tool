Another or Other? Fun with Words!
=================================

Instructions: Hello, Super Readers! Let's learn when to use 'another' and 'other'. 'Another' means one more of the same kind. 'Other' means different ones or more than one! Follow the directions in each box. Have fun!

Page 1: Let's Learn 'Another' and 'Other'!
------------------------------------------
1. 🎨 Fun Design: Bright blue sky background with fluffy white clouds, a cheerful sun in the corner, and a colorful border of crayons and pencils.

1. 'Another' Means One More!
----------------------------
1. Look at the picture. Draw *another* one just like it in the empty box!
2. [Image: A single red apple] Draw another red apple.
3. [Image: A single blue t-shirt] Draw another blue t-shirt.
4. [Image: A calendar showing 'Monday'] Draw another calendar showing 'Tuesday'.
5. [Image: A single yellow house with a red roof] Draw another yellow house with a red roof.

2. 'Other' Means Different Ones!
--------------------------------
1. Look at the first picture in each row. Then, circle all the *other* items that are different or more of the same kind!
2. [Image: One happy child] Circle the *other* children: [Image: Two more happy children playing]
3. [Image: One red shoe] Circle the *other* clothes: [Image: A blue hat, green pants, yellow socks]
4. [Image: One big tree] Circle the *other* plants: [Image: A flower, a bush, a small plant]
5. [Image: A calendar showing 'January'] Circle the *other* months: [Image: Calendar showing 'February', Calendar showing 'March', Calendar showing 'April']

Page 2: Choosing the Right Word!
--------------------------------
1. 🌈 Fun Design: Light green grassy background with a rainbow arching over, and a border of smiling stars and moons.

3. 'Another' or 'Other'? Choose the Best Word!
----------------------------------------------
1. Read the sentence. Circle the best word to complete it!
2. I ate one cookie. Can I have ___ one? A) another B) other
3. These are my shoes. What ___ clothes do I need? A) another B) other
4. Today is Monday. What ___ days are in the week? A) another B) other
5. I see one big house. Are there ___ houses on the street? A) another B) other
6. We planted one tree. Let's plant ___ tree! A) another B) other

4. Draw and Write!
------------------
1. Draw a picture in the box and finish the sentence using 'another' or 'other'.
2. Draw a picture of fruit. Then complete: I want __________ piece of fruit. (Hint: one more!)
3. Draw a picture of toys. Then complete: I like to play with __________ toys. (Hint: many different ones!)

5. Finish the Sentences!
------------------------
1. Fill in the blank with 'another' or 'other'.
2. It's sunny today! I hope we have ___ sunny day tomorrow.
3. We read one book. Let's read some ___ books.
4. January is a month. What ___ months are in the year?
5. I have one red block. Can I have ___ red block?
6. My friend has one cat. Do you have ___ pets?
